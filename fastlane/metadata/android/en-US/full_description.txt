Open HKA is an open-source mobile client for the IWI HKA timetable system,
which allows you to view your timetable on your smartphone or tablet.

The app allows customization of almost any aspect of your timetable,
including multiple themes and a dark mode.

Easily add multiple profiles with separate settings and timetables.
Use Notifications to show information about the next lesson.
Enjoy a fast and smooth experience with Open HKA.

Main features of the app:
- Viewing your timetable
- Viewing RSS feeds (News)
- Viewing events related to the Hochschule Karlsruhe (HKA)
- Searching and finding people at Hochschule Karlsruhe (HKA)
- Viewing the occupation of study places
- Viewing the mensa menus

This app is specialized for the Hochschule Karlsruhe (HKA).
Support for other timetables can not be guaranteed.
This app is open-source! Help improve it!
